DOG BREEDS

German Shepherd Description
German Shepherd's are known to be large and muscular dogs. They are known for being protective, alert and strong.
Golden Retriever Description
Golden Retrievers are medium sized dogs with gold fur. They are known to be very friendly and gentle.
Pomeranian Description
Pomeranian's are small dogs. They are known for being playful and friendly.
Chihuahua Description
Chihuahua's are small dogs. They are loud yet intelligent and loving..
Bulldog Description
Golden Retrievers are medium sized dogs with gold fur. They are known to be very friendly and gentle.
Corgi Description
Corgi's are medium sized dogs. They are known for being loving to their owners, gentle and friendly.
Siberian Husky Description
Siberian Huskies are said to be descendants of wolves. They are large dogs as well. They are known for being independent, friendly and stubborn.
Poodle Description
Poodles are small to medium sized dogs. They are known for being smart, energetic and easy to train.
Chow Chow Description
Chow Chow's are large dogs. They are known for being not so friendly but loyal and independent.
Dachshund Description
Dachshund's are medium sized dogs. We often know them for having a body that looks like a hot dog. They are also independent and smart..
